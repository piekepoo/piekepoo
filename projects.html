Seazo



Summary

A game of being a little fish avoiding trash in the sea. The higher your progress, the more polluted the sea will be. Developed with Processing, Java programming language.

Concept

Always wanted to be a little fish swimming in a large sea full of pollution and waste? Probably not. Well, you’re out of luck then due to humans’ recent polluting activities. Today you are a little fishy trying to find its way through this polluted ocean looking for food. Try to gather as much food as possible to increase points and avoid floating plastics and metals. Don’t get eaten by the shark that’s chasing you!

Motivation

We wanted to draw attention to sea pollution. Seas are filled with all different kinds of trash and it’s putting a lot of pressure on sea creatures, making it hard for them to survive and for many species to risk extinction. The chances of survival are becoming more limited. On the one hand there is the increasing risk of being subject to this pollution: getting stuck in plastic waste or getting injured by trash cans and on the other hand the amount of food that’s left is decreasing immensely due to overfishing. There is no way out, which is why there is no winning in this game. It’s all about surviving the longest!

Implementation

We made this game in Java with Processing. The animations we drew them ourselves and we just changed the positions of the images in the code.
