  • About me


    Welcome! My name is Pieke Heijmans and I have an academic background in Media Studies (BA) and Media Technology (MSc). I’ve done several group projects and more specifically I am interested in creating content with a funny and ironic twist.

    I was born in Nijmegen and after highschool I moved to groningen to study psychology. After realizing I was missing a creative element in my career path, I decided to study Media studies in Amsterdam. During my studies I followed a minor in programming and this is where I came to the conclusion that I wanted to develop my technological skills and combine that with creating creative content. As a result, I followed a Master’s in Media Technology (MT), where I combined technology, science and scientific writing with creativity. MT allowed me to work on different group projects and as a result I’ve been involved in several cool group projects of which some were displayed during school exhibitions. These projects can be reviewed under the projects section.

    As of lately, I’ve started with web-development and web-design. I am still learning, but I’m enjoying being able to code something that directly displays output. This website is my first project, but possibly more will follow.
